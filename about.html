About Me

I'm a sophomore at UBC Sauder studying Business, Computer Science and Statistics, born and raised in Vancouver with roots in Guangzhou, China.

I've always been obsessed with how numbers and data shape our world, which has drawn me towards financial technologies and quantitative finance, specifically how technology and artificial intelligence can reshape financial systems and capital market flow.

I'm competitive by nature and I'm always up for a challenge or a good time, having honed my strategy and product skills across case competitions, hackathons, product competitions, and datathons.

Outside of my academics and career, I love hiking in the summer, dissecting lyrics, film and art, going to concerts (BTS this summer!) and capturing moments with my friends.
